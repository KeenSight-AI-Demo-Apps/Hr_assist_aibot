Q: Is acupuncture covered?
A: Yes, when deemed medically necessary and in-network.

Q: How do I find an in-network doctor?
A: Use the provider search on the insurer's website or call customer service.

Q: Are therapy and counseling covered?
A: Yes, both in-person and telehealth options are available.

Q: What does EAP include?
A: Counseling, legal help, financial tools, and more — all free and confidential.

Q: Is orthodontics for adults covered?
A: Yes, with lifetime benefit caps.

Q: What happens if I miss Open Enrollment?
A: You must wait for a Qualifying Life Event.

Q: How do I access telemedicine?
A: Use our partner app or web portal — see HR for login help.
